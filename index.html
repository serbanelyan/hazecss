Reimagining Framework Modularity

Traditional frameworks often promise ease of development, yet they fall short in addressing the diverse needs of modern web projects; I've designed Haze to address these complexities with a unique twist.

Haze breaks free from convention by offering a unique approach to modularity. Structured into three distinct flavors, Haze empowers developers at every stage of the website creation journey. Begin with Haze's base framework, a minimalist, class-less solution that breathes life into HTML with clean, modern styles. Elevate your designs with Haze+ for enhanced layout control and utility, or dive into the world of intricate design elements with Haze++.
